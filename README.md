# Enterprise Sales Performance & Revenue Intelligence Dashboard
## Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Power BI solution developed using an enterprise-scale sales dataset as part of FP20 Analytics Challenge 35.

The objective was to evaluate sales performance, pipeline health, conversion efficiency, forecasting reliability, and revenue risk exposure across regions, industries, and sales representatives.

Key Business Questions Addressed

How healthy and balanced is the sales pipeline?

Where do the largest conversion drop-offs occur?

How does sales rep activity influence win rates?

Is revenue concentrated geographically or diversified?

How reliable is the revenue forecast?

Are there early warning signals for high-risk deals?

## Dashboard Structure
Page 1 – Performance Overview

High-level revenue trends, win rate, pipeline distribution, and industry performance.

Page 2 – Pipeline & Rep Intelligence

Stage-level conversion analysis, sales cycle comparison, rep performance evaluation, and activity impact assessment.

Page 3 – Risk & Forecasting

Revenue concentration analysis, high-risk deal monitoring, product win rate analysis, and forecast vs actual comparison.


## Key Insights

Revenue growth is strong but heavily concentrated in one region.

Significant attrition occurs between early and late pipeline stages.

Higher sales engagement correlates with improved win rates.

Forecast reliability depends on improving mid-stage progression.

Seasonal revenue acceleration impacts planning and target setting.
